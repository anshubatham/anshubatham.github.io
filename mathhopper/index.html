Common Questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Yes — Math Hopper is completely free to download and play on Android. The app is supported by non-intrusive ads via Google AdMob. No purchases are required to access the core gameplay, leaderboards, or achievements.

Leaderboards are powered by Firebase Realtime Database. As you complete challenges and earn points, your score is updated in real time. Players from around the world are ranked by their cumulative scores, and you can filter by country or region.

Core gameplay works offline. However, an internet connection is required to sync your scores to the global leaderboard, access your profile from a new device, and receive achievement updates in real time.

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. We collect only what's necessary: your player name, country, scores, and achievements. We never sell your data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

You have the right to request complete deletion of your data at any time. Simply email us at batham925@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and your registered player name. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email once your data has been permanently removed.

Absolutely. Math Hopper is designed for players of all ages, including children. We comply with Google Play's Families Policy and applicable children's privacy laws. Ads shown are family-safe. Parental consent for data collection from children under 13 is handled in accordance with COPPA guidelines. See our Privacy Policy for our Children's Privacy section.
